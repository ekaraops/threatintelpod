## New Tools, Techniques, and Tactics
*   **AI-Driven Zero-Day Discovery:** Using Large Language Models (LLMs) to scan open-source code for unknown vulnerabilities.
*   **Automated Exploitation Scripting:** AI models that not only find a bug but also write the code to exploit it (e.g., Blind SQL injection).
*   **Transshipment:** A tactic where sanctioned goods are sold to a neutral country (like Turkey) before being moved to the final sanctioned destination (Russia).
*   **High-Bandwidth Point-to-Point Comms:** Using commercial Wi-Fi bridges (like Ubiquiti) to transmit live drone video in combat zones.

## Detection & Defense Insights
*   **AI-Enhanced Fuzzing/Logic Analysis:** AI is moving beyond simple pattern matching to "thinking" through the logic of source code to find subtle bugs.
*   **Supply Chain Due Diligence:** Companies must implement stricter checks on wholesalers to prevent "on-selling" to sanctioned entities.
*   **Vulnerability Management:** The speed of AI discovery means the window to patch critical software (like the Linux kernel) is shrinking.
*   **Signal for SOCs:** Increased use of commercial networking gear in conflict zones may signal shifts in adversary communication tactics.
